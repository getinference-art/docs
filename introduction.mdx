When travelers ask ChatGPT, Google AI Mode, Perplexity, Claude, or Gemini questions like "best hotels in Paris" or "where to stay near the Eiffel Tower," does your brand show up in the answer?

GeoTravel.ai helps you find out — and gives you the tools to improve.

## What GeoTravel.ai Does

GeoTravel.ai monitors how AI platforms talk about your hotel, resort, or destination. Every day, it sends real travel queries to the major AI search engines and checks:

- **Does the AI mention your brand by name?** When someone asks for hotel recommendations, is your property in the answer?
- **Does it link to your website?** AI answers often include citations — GeoTravel.ai detects whether yours is among them.
- **Does it show you on the map?** Google AI Mode and AI Overviews sometimes display a map with places. We check if your property appears there.
- **How do you compare to competitors?** Your Share of Voice score tells you what percentage of relevant queries feature your brand versus others in your market.

Based on all of this, GeoTravel.ai gives you clear, actionable recommendations to strengthen your presence in AI-generated answers.
