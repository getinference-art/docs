# Score Benchmarks

A quick reference for all the scores, grades, and metrics you'll see across the platform.

---

## Share of Voice (SOV)

The percentage of AI responses that mention your brand.

| SOV Range | Assessment | What It Means |
|-----------|------------|---------------|
| 20%+ | **Strong** | AI regularly recommends you |
| 10–20% | **Moderate** | You appear but inconsistently |
| 5–10% | **Limited** | Occasionally mentioned |
| Under 5% | **Minimal** | AI rarely mentions you |

---

## AI Visibility Index (AIV)

A 0–100 score combining how often you're mentioned, where you appear in the response, and how strong the content around your mention is.

| Score | Grade | What It Means |
|-------|-------|---------------|
| 90–100 | **S** | Exceptional — AI treats you as the authority |
| 80–89 | **A** | Excellent — strong, consistent presence |
| 60–79 | **B** | Good — solid foundation, room to grow |
| 40–59 | **C** | Fair — inconsistent presence |
| 20–39 | **D** | Below average — significant gaps |
| 0–19 | **F** | Needs work — barely visible to AI |

---

## Relevance Score (0–100)

How strongly AI associates your property with the queries you're tracking.

| Range | Label | What It Means |
|-------|-------|---------------|
| 80–100 | **Excellent** | AI strongly associates you with these topics |
| 60–79 | **Good** | Solid presence, room for optimization |
| 40–59 | **Fair** | You appear inconsistently |
| 0–39 | **Needs Improvement** | AI rarely considers you relevant |

---

## Influence Score (0–100)

When you are mentioned, how much space and prominence you get in the response.

Uses the same scale as Relevance Score (Excellent / Good / Fair / Needs Improvement).

---

## Opportunity Score (0–100)

Used in Semantic Opportunities to rank where you should focus your efforts.

| Range | Label | What It Means |
|-------|-------|---------------|
| 80+ | **Exceptional** | High gap, low competition, good demand — act now |
| 60–79 | **Strong** | Worth pursuing actively |
| 40–59 | **Moderate** | Consider based on available resources |
| Under 40 | **Low Priority** | Probably not worth the effort right now |

---

## Relevance Score for Conversation Threads (0–100)

How important a community thread is for your AI visibility strategy.

| Range | Label | What It Means |
|-------|-------|---------------|
| 70+ | **Highly Relevant** | Prioritize engagement |
| 50–69 | **Worth Reviewing** | Review and decide |
| 30–49 | **Moderate** | Lower priority |
| Under 30 | **Low Priority** | Skip unless you have capacity |

---

## Content Quality Signal Impact

Research-backed impact on AI visibility when these elements appear on your website.

| Signal | Impact | What to Do |
|--------|--------|------------|
| **Statistics & data** | +41.2% | Add numbers: room counts, ratings, distances, prices |
| **Quotations & testimonials** | +38.5% | Add guest reviews, press quotes, expert endorsements |
| **Technical terms** | +18.6% | Use industry-specific language instead of generic descriptions |

<Note>
These percentages come from the peer-reviewed GEO (Generative Engine Optimization) research paper. They represent average visibility improvement when these content types are present.
</Note>
