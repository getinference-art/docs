The Competitive Gap Analysis answers one straightforward question: **When someone asks AI about your industry, does AI mention you or not - and why?**

## The Core Concept: Wins vs. Losses

Every time a tracking run executes, your prompts are sent to multiple AI engines (ChatGPT, Gemini, Perplexity, Google AI). Each response is then evaluated:

- **Win (Mentioned)** - AI mentioned your brand in its answer
- **Loss (Not Mentioned)** - AI did not mention your brand. It mentioned competitors or other brands instead

The analysis then determines **why** you won or lost in each case.

---

## What You See

### Win Rate

Your headline metric showing how often AI mentions your brand across all analyzed prompts.

| Metric | What It Means |
|--------|---------------|
| **Mentioned** (green) | Number of prompts where AI mentioned your brand |
| **Not Mentioned** (red) | Number of prompts where AI did not mention your brand |
| **Win Rate** | Mentioned divided by Total, shown as a percentage |

**Example:** Out of 14 analyzed prompts, your brand was mentioned in 11 responses and missing from 3. That is a 78.6% win rate.

### Why You Win

When your brand IS mentioned, the analysis identifies what is working in your favor. These are your competitive advantages - the things you should continue doing. Examples include:

- Strong positioning as the recognized leader in your space
- High perceived relevance and clarity of what you offer
- Brand name that directly matches common search queries

### Key Gaps to Address

When your brand is NOT mentioned, the analysis pinpoints what is missing. These are your action items. Examples include:

- No dedicated page explicitly addressing a specific query topic
- Missing third-party discussions and endorsements
- Lack of structured content that AI can easily retrieve

<Tip>
The Gap Analysis complements your Share of Voice score. SOV tells you **where you stand**. Gap Analysis tells you **what to do about it**.
</Tip>

## How It Works

<Steps>

### AI responses are collected

Every tracking run sends your prompts to multiple AI engines and captures the full response, including which brands were mentioned, how many times, and which websites were cited.

### The most valuable responses are selected for deep analysis

The system prioritizes responses where competitors beat you, followed by emerging market opportunities, and then your wins. Every prompt gets at least one analysis so nothing is skipped entirely.

### Each response is analyzed for patterns

For wins, the system identifies your strengths and what you should keep doing. For losses, it identifies what the winning competitor did differently and what specific improvements you should make.

### Results are deduplicated

The same prompt may be analyzed across multiple tracking runs. Only the latest analysis per prompt is kept, so you always see the most current competitive picture without inflated numbers.

### Findings appear in your briefing

The top winning factors and top gaps are surfaced in your Executive Briefing. You can also view the full Gap Analysis for complete detail.

</Steps>

---

## How Wins and Losses Are Determined

A "win" or "loss" is determined by who got the most mentions or citations in each AI response:

- **Mentions Win** - Your brand name appeared more times than any competitor in the AI response
- **Citations Win** - The AI cited your website more than any competitor's website in its sources

<Note>
Coverage increases with each tracking run. If you recently added new prompts, they will be included in the next analysis cycle. Over time, every prompt in your workspace gets analyzed.
</Note>

## How to Improve Your Win Rate

<AccordionGroup>

<Accordion title="Quick Wins (days to weeks)">
1. **Update your Google Business Profile** - Complete all fields, add photos, and respond to reviews
2. **Add FAQ pages** that directly answer your tracked prompts
3. **Include statistics and data points** on your website such as room counts, ratings, and distances to landmarks
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Medium-Term (weeks to months)">
4. **Get cited by authoritative sources** - Tourism boards, review sites, and industry publications
5. **Create content with quotes and testimonials** that AI can reference
6. **Use proper structured data markup** so AI can easily extract information about your brand
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Long-Term (months)">
7. **Build brand mentions across the web** - Forums, social media, and press coverage
8. **Publish original research or data** that AI models will pick up over time
9. **Maintain consistent content updates** - Freshness signals matter to AI engines
</Accordion>

</AccordionGroup>

## Understanding Confidence Levels

Each analysis comes with a confidence score from 0 to 100%. Higher confidence means the system is more certain about why you won or lost.

- **80%+** - Very reliable findings
- **60-80%** - Solid directional guidance
- **Below 60%** - Treat as directional hints rather than certainties

---

## How Often Is It Updated?

- Gap analysis runs **automatically after each tracking run**
- The briefing always shows the **latest result per prompt**
- Running more tracking runs means more prompts get analyzed and better overall coverage
- Results are cached for 7 days to avoid redundant analysis of the same patterns

<Warning>
Not all prompts are analyzed in every single run. The system prioritizes the most valuable analyses first and ensures every prompt gets covered over successive runs. Check back after a few tracking cycles for complete coverage.
</Warning>
